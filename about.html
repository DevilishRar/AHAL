ABOUT AHAL

Experience a complete life cycle in Minecraft, where every minute counts in your one-hour journey.

THE CONCEPT

AHAL (A Hour A Life) transforms the traditional Minecraft experience into an intense one-hour life simulation. Every minute represents a year in your character's life, creating a unique and challenging gameplay experience.

COMMUNITY

Join a vibrant community of players who shape the world together. Every life leaves a lasting impact on the server's history and environment.
